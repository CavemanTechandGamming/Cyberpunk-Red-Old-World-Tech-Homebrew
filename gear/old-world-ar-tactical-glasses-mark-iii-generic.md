# Old World AR Tactical Glasses — Mark III (Generic)

**Short description:** Legendary, dock-only Old World AR glasses—**non-functional** without a **Mark III cranial dock**. Full Agent stack and brutal integration when seated; **five** stacked bonuses including **Electronics / Security Tech +5**.

This is the **baseline Mark III** loadout. Role-specific variants tune overlays without changing the overall power level. In this homebrew line, **Mark III** is **late-game / legendary** gear and **requires** `cyberwear/old-world-cranial-ar-dock-mark-iii.md` to operate.

## Description

These frames are not “wearable salvage” anymore. Someone—maybe a desperate tech, maybe a Militech ghost—**gutted the internal power plant** and **hard-locked the bus** to a **cranial Mark III dock**. Off the skull, they are **dead glass**: no HUD, no Agent, no bonuses. Seated in an **Old World Cranial AR Dock — Mark III Interface**, they wake: power and data ride the implant’s regulated spine, overlays run at bandwidth lower marks never touch, and the rig finally stops apologizing for being external.

You still are not getting full cybereyes—but docked Mark III is the closest these lenses get.

**Operational requirement:** **Must** be **docked** in a compatible **Mark III cranial implant** to function. There is **no** standalone battery workflow; undocked Mark III glasses do **not** grant any listed modifiers.

Highly valued, absurdly rare, and a good way to get disappeared if the wrong corp notices you wearing them.
