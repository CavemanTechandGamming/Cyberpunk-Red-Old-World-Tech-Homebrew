# Old World AR Tactical Glasses — Mark II (Solo)

**Short description:** Mark II pre-Collapse AR glasses tuned for fights—full Agent replacement, improved optics vs Mark I, and a HUD that favors target discrimination and weapon solutions.

## Description

A rare piece of Old World Tech, the **Mark II (Solo)** augmented reality tactical glasses refine the Mark I combat loadout. Worn on the face, they are intended as a **complete substitute for a pocket Agent**: comms, Data Pool access, apps, scheduling, recording, and ShortLink-style control live in the rig, so you do not need to carry a discrete Agent **while the glasses are powered and on your head**.

This variant biases the HUD toward **lethal work**: faster ammo and stance hints, cleaner priority tags on movement, and less HUD stutter when rounds are in the air—tactical assist, not a targeting computer wired into your brain. Optics stay modest but improved: better contrast in smoke and strobing light, **smoother zoom** for IDs and sight pictures—no implanted-grade suite.

They are **not** full cyberoptics. Unlike implanted cyberware, the system is entirely external—making it vulnerable to damage, interference, and theft. It will never match wired-in optics for speed, integration, or raw capability.

Power is easier to live with than on Mark I: **plan on recharging about every two to three days** under full HUD and Agent load, or **push toward a week** if you idle overlays between jobs—but let the battery go flat and the whole rig powers down, modifiers and all.

Highly valued by Edgerunners who want capability without chrome, and hunted by those who recognize their origin.

**Cost:** 1,250 eb (homebrew; mid-game tier)

## Custom Modifiers

While worn and operational, the glasses grant:

- **Reflexes:** +4
- **Attack rolls:** +2
- **Aimed Shot:** +2
