# Old World Pitlane Spanjack — Mark III

**Short description:** **Legendary-refit** **Pitlane Spanjack**—low-profile high-reach hydraulic floor jack with mil-surplus discipline on the ram, overbuilt wheels, and a pump that still feels like cheating. **Speed** stays **10** (bicycle-tier Companion parity).

## Long description

**Mark III** is not “a nicer jack.” It is what you build when you steal the best Old World hydraulics still on pallets—seals rated for temperature swings, ram straight enough for paranoia, and a frame that forgives being used as an improvised battering ram when the bay door jams.

Fiction-wise, this is the jack a convoy lead tech chains to the flatbed because trust is torque measured in tons.

**Appearance:** Frame Cerakoted or ceramic-feel black; ram boot clean; wheels matched and bearing-silent. Maybe a faint Old World part stamp under the paint. Saddle pad dense rubber that does not split when you look at it wrong. Still rolls at bicycle-tier Speed—physics does not care about your feelings.

**Cost:** **GM / table.** **Mark III** can punch above its size in *your* economy if legendary shop tools are rare—still nowhere near a real car unless you say otherwise.

**Suggested price:** 900 eb — *baseline for balance; change freely to fit your campaign.*

**Structural damage points:** 20

**Seats:** 1

**Rooms:** 1

**Speed:** 10
