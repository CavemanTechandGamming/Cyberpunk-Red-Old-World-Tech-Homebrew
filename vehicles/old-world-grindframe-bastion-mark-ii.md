# Old World Grindframe Bastion — Mark II

**Short description:** **Refurbished Grindframe Bastion**: **4WD**, **turbodiesel**, **full-frame** three-row **tank**—suspension sorted, cooling honest, interior **sanitized** back to “family road trip” instead of “wet dog and regret.” **Factory beefy off-road bumpers** are straightened or refinished, not deleted. One that starts when you twist the key the **first** time.

## Long description

**Mark II** is what happens when someone with **shop access** and **patience** treats the **Bastion** like **infrastructure** instead of a **disposable shell**. Bushings, **brake** lines, **radiator** stack, **transfer-case** **seals**, and the **gremlin** in the transmission bus get addressed **before** the paint touch-up. The **third row** stops being a **storage grave**; the **AC** stops **negotiating**. The **turbodiesel** still rattles windows at idle—love it or leave it. It is still the same **rolling monument**—**eight seats**, **truck bones**, **four-wheel drive**, **rear opening** you could **sleep** in if you had to—but it drives like the Collapse **paused** mid-apology.

Fiction-wise, this is the **crew vehicle** a fixer loans when the job needs **bodies** and **gear** in one **envelope** without looking like a **military** convoy. It will not outrun an AV; it will **absorb** small arms and **keep** the kids in the back from asking questions you cannot answer.

**Appearance:** **Body** gaps even; **trim** replaced where it used to **flap** at speed. **Glass** might still carry **pitting**, but **cracks** are **patched** or **swapped**. **Factory steel bumpers**—brush guard and rear step—get sandblasted, primed, or powder-coated so they read deliberate again. **Wheels** match; **tires** are **depth-legal** instead of **faith-based**. Interior **carpet** is **shampooed** or **replaced**; **headliner** no longer **sags** like a **tired** mouth. Someone **deleted** the **factory** **DVD** ceiling screen and **wired** a **ShortLink** **head unit**—or left it **stock** to **blend**. From the curb: **respectable** **suburban** **war barge**, not a **scream**.

**Cost:** **GM / table.** **Mark II** should track **mid-tier** **large** SUVs and **utility** wagons in *your* economy—more than a **beater** coupe, less than a **fresh** **armored** limo unless you say otherwise.
