# Old World Redline Viceroy — Mark II

**Short description:** **Refurbished Redline Viceroy**—**rear-wheel-drive**, **350-class** **V8** **2+2** coupe with the upscale trim’s confidence restored: suspension sorted, cooling honest, interior sanitized back to “Saturday night” instead of “evidence bag.” Still **RWD only**—no **AWD** bolted on.

## Long description

**Mark II** is what happens when someone with shop access and patience treats the **Viceroy** like a car again instead of a project that owns you. Bushings, brake lines, radiator, diff seals, and the gremlin in the carb or EFI map get addressed before the wax. The **small-block** still barks through factory or period-correct exhaust—torque you feel in the seat rails.

Fiction-wise, this is the coupe a solo shows up in when the job needs style and escape lanes—not subtle, not apologetic, definitely **rear-wheel drive**.

**Appearance:** Panel gaps even; trim replaced where sun killed it. Glass clear enough to see regret. Wheels match; tires round instead of faith-based. T-tops resealed or roof painted to match. Interior carpet deep-cleaned; dash bulbs swapped so gauges lie consistently. From the curb: respectable chrome and ego.

**Cost:** **GM / table.** **Mark II** should track **mid-tier** muscle and performance coupes in *your* economy—more than a beater hatch, less than a fresh import supercar unless you say otherwise.

**Suggested price:** 19,000 eb — *baseline for balance; change freely to fit your campaign.*

**Structural damage points:** 52

**Seats:** 4

**Rooms:** 1

**Speed:** 77

**Drivetrain:** Rear-wheel drive

**Powerplant:** Small-block V8 (~350 cu. in. class—factory line spec for this homebrew)
