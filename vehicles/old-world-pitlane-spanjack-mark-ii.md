# Old World Pitlane Spanjack — Mark II

**Short description:** **Refurbished Pitlane Spanjack**—low-profile high-reach hydraulic floor jack with seals honest, fluid clean enough to trust, and wheels that roll in a straight line. **Speed** stays **10** (bicycle-tier Companion parity—still hand-rolled).

## Long description

**Mark II** is what happens when a crew treats the **Spanjack** like a tool instead of a disposable lever. Seals replaced or reseated, ram polished where it matters, pump rebuilt so the handle does not fight you, and the release valve stops weeping on your boots.

Fiction-wise, this is the jack a tech brings when the job is not allowed to fail—subframe swap, armor skid shim, get the damn wheel off before the raid starts.

**Appearance:** Frame painted or powder-coated so it reads deliberate again—racing red, pit yellow, or gunmetal grey. Saddle pad replaced; wheels matched. Maybe a small calibration sticker from a shop that still had liability insurance. Still rolls at bicycle-tier Speed when you push it.

**Cost:** **GM / table.** **Mark II** should sit above salvage junk, below a monowheel carrier board in *your* economy unless you say otherwise.

**Suggested price:** 350 eb — *baseline for balance; change freely to fit your campaign.*

**Structural damage points:** 16

**Seats:** 1

**Rooms:** 1

**Speed:** 10
