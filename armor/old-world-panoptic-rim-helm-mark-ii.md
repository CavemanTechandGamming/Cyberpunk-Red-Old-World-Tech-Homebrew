## Long description

**Mark II** panoptic helms shipped to corporate rapid-response and VIP protection teams before the Collapse: upgraded ceramic band, factory-sealed visor gasket, and rim firmware that held calibration longer in heat and noise. Cold spots in the composite are rarer; audio hiss is lower if the mesh was serviced.

Still not cyberoptics—projection only, no neural pipe. Neck and below stay uncovered without other gear.

**Appearance:** Cleaner paint lines—charcoal, black, OD—with less chip at the brim on well-stored lots. The visor yellows slower; some Mark IIs use slightly tinted grey-green panes. The HUD rim tracks tighter—ghosting shows as faint double images only when power droops. Ear cups use denser foam; vents are smaller and more precise. The chin strap is sometimes reinforced with a carbon tab—less stretch over years.
